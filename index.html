Our principal,

Mr Simphiwe Kuze

Our principal, Simphiwe Kuze, has been the principal at Mzamomtsha primary since Jan 2018- Present. Prior to that he was a Deputy principal at Manzothombo SSS from April 2004 - Dec 2017.

During this time he was alos a History NCS marker, Chief marker, Internal Moderator from Nov 2001 - Jun 2019. He went to Archie Velile Senior Secondary in Dimbaza, Eastern Cape.He attented University of Western cape. The school motto being "striving for success" we want each learner to reach their full potential.
Always Striving for Success in everything we do.
